JOB CATEGORIZATION & DIFFERENTIATION FEATURES
=============================================

This document outlines the new features implemented for job posting and categorization in the Connecta application. These features apply to both Individual Freelancer jobs and Collabo Team projects.

1. JOB TYPES (Engagement Models)
--------------------------------
Differentiates the nature of the engagement between the client and the talent.

- Full-time: Standard long-term employment.
- Part-time: Partial hours engagement.
- Contract: Fixed-term agreement.
- Freelance: Project-based independence.
- One-time: Single task or "gig" (New).
- Monthly: Retainer-based recurring work (New).
- Permanent: Indefinite employment (New).
- Adhoc: As-needed basis (New).

2. JOB SCOPE (Location Strategy)
--------------------------------
Defines the geographical reach of the job.

- Within Country (Local): Targeted at talent within the client's country (e.g., Nigeria).
- International (Abroad): Open to global talent.

3. LOCATION TYPE (Workplace)
----------------------------
Defines where the work takes place.

- Remote: Work from anywhere.
- Onsite (Physical): Work at a specific location.
- Hybrid: Mix of remote and onsite.

4. CATEGORIES & NICHES (Industry Specialization)
------------------------------------------------
Comprehensive list of industries with specific sub-categories.

A. Technology & Programming
   - Web Development, Mobile Development, Software Engineering, Data Science, DevOps, etc.

B. Design & Creative
   - Graphic Design, UI/UX, Branding, 3D Modeling, Animation, etc.

C. Marketing & Sales
   - Digital Marketing, SEO, Content Marketing, Social Media, etc.

D. Business & Finance
   - Accounting, Project Management, Virtual Assistance, Legal, HR, etc.

E. Writing & Translation
   - Copywriting, Technical Writing, Translation, Editing, etc.

F. Hospitality & Events (New Niche)
   - Hotel Management, Event Planning, Catering, Travel Planning, etc.

G. Health & Fitness (New Niche)
   - Personal Training, Nutrition, Wellness Coaching, Telehealth, etc.

H. Education & Training
   - Tutoring, Course Creation, Language Instruction, etc.

I. Other
   - Allows for manual entry if the category is not listed.

5. DURATION SPECIFICATION
-------------------------
Granular control over the expected timeline of the project.

- Value: Numeric input (e.g., "3", "6").
- Unit: Days, Weeks, Months, Years.

IMPLEMENTATION SUMMARY
----------------------
- Backend: Schema updated to support all new fields (`jobType`, `jobScope`, `niche`, `duration`).
- Frontend: `PostJobScreen` updated with interactive "Chips" and selection lists for intuitive user experience.
- UI/UX: Modern selection interface replacing manual text inputs.
